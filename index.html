About the Site

Glacier View Commons site was the headquarters for the Everett School District as the “Educational Resource Center” for nearly 50 years. The site includes the building and approximately 8 acres in the Glacier View Neighborhood at 4700 Colby Ave.

Vision

As a true community commons, community uses for this site are almost endless. Imagine these activities.

  • Sustainable gardens and training center
  • Social-purpose company incubator
  • Transition town resource cooperative
  • Indigenous cultural center
  • Senior citizen community support and engagement opportunities
  • Youth in Action/Unleash the Brilliance peer-to-peer counseling and young adult development
  • Community networks communications node
  • Neighborhood-based STEM learning and applied site projects
  • Community alternative healing center
  • Housing Hope / Hope College training extension
  • Many, many more of your ideas!

How You Can Help

We need your participation and involvement right now as the Everett School District determines the future of the surplus Educational Service Center building and grounds at 4700 Colby Ave, located in the Glacier View Neighborhood.

Together we can transform the Educational Resource Center into a thriving community center where we can all learn, work, and play
